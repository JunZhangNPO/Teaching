### Preparation
- Stakeholders are assigned roles and make decisions as part of a board discussion for a nonprofit organization.  
- Groups of **4 to 7 people** are ideal.  
- Each participant takes on a **specific role**.  
- Some **individual preparation time** is provided before the group discussion begins.

### Discussion
- **Focus on completing the task.**  
- **Monitor the group’s progress.**  
- **Encourage participation** from everyone, especially those who haven't spoken much.

### Reporting
- Assign a **reporter** — ideally someone who has spoken less during the discussion.  
- Reports can take different forms:
  - Oral briefing  
  - Mock press conference  
- **Q&A session** may follow the report.
