# Using Online Resources for Teaching

## 1. Explore Social Media Platforms

- Search your course topic on YouTube and TikTok.
- Review existing content related to the topic (e.g., market analysis).
- Identify what types of videos are posted and what audiences care about.
- Analyze which videos are most popular and how the content is delivered (format, structure, tone, visuals).

## 2. Use AI Tools

- Ask AI tools to generate content ideas for your course topic.
- Consider the appropriate depth and delivery style for your student level (e.g., undergraduate vs. master’s students).

## 3. Conduct a Google Search

- Look for recent and relevant news articles.
- Identify current trends and developments in the nonprofit industry.
- Incorporate timely examples into class discussions.
